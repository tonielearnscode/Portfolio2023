Resume

A self care app that uses methods by psychologists

I created a health app that focuses on psychologically backed coping mechanisms such as making quick mindful observations. This will help users achieve their long and short term goals. Other self care apps just make adding goals quick and count calories but it isn't mindful or easy to be consistant in. As a result, most users fall out of their own goals. Take a look at my redesign!

Project Background

Create a self care app to help people have healthier and consistent lifestyles. We need to figure out if the app is easy to use for customers. We would like to learn the challenges that people face when using health apps and how we can help fix those challenges.
